Placements

JECRC Foundation is committed to providing top-notch placement opportunities to its students, enabling them to build successful careers. We have a robust career development cell that works closely with industry partners to understand their needs and provide students with the right set of skills and knowledge required to excel in their chosen career paths. Our students receive extensive training and mentoring, allowing them to become industry-ready professionals. We have a proud track record of high placement rates, with our students being placed in renowned companies in India and abroad. Our focus on holistic development and personalized attention to each student’s career goals sets us apart from other institutions, making us the preferred choice for students seeking a successful career.
